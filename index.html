About Chahia

Our story and passion for Algerian cuisine

Our Story

Chahia, meaning "appetite" in Arabic, was born from a passion for sharing authentic Algerian flavors with the world. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, maintaining the true essence of Algerian cuisine.

We carefully select the finest ingredients and spices to create dishes that transport you to the streets of Algiers, the mountains of Kabylie, and the Sahara desert.

Our Mission

To provide an authentic taste of Algeria while educating our customers about the rich culinary traditions of this beautiful North African country.
